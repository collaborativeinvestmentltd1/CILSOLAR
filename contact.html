Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I get a solar quote?

Fill out the contact form or call us at 0800 SOLAR CIL. Our team will assess your needs and provide a customized quote within 24 hours.

Do you offer installation services?

Yes! We provide professional installation across Nigeria. Our certified technicians ensure safe and efficient setup of your solar system.

What warranty do you offer?

We offer manufacturer warranties on all products plus our installation warranty. Panels come with 25-year warranty, inverters 5-10 years, and batteries 5-10 years.

Can I finance my solar purchase?

Yes! We offer flexible payment plans and financing options. Contact our sales team to discuss financing solutions that fit your budget.

How long does installation take?

Residential installations typically take 1-3 days. Commercial installations vary based on system size. We'll provide a timeline during consultation.

Do you service existing solar systems?

Yes! We provide maintenance and repair services for all solar systems, regardless of original installer. Contact our support team for assistance.
