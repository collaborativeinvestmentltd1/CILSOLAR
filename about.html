About CIL Solar Energy Solutions

We are a division of Collaborative Investment Ltd, dedicated to professional management of solar energy across all sectors — from agribusiness to hospitality, IT to diaspora asset management.

Our Mission

To provide professional solar energy management across every sector, making clean, reliable power accessible to businesses, industries, and individuals while supporting sustainable development.

Our Vision

To become Nigeria's most trusted solar energy partner, powering businesses and homes across 20+ sectors with innovative, reliable, and scalable solar solutions.

Our Story

CIL Solar Energy Solutions was born from a simple observation: Nigeria has abundant sunshine, yet businesses and homes struggle with unreliable power. We saw an opportunity to bridge this gap by providing professional solar management across diverse sectors.

As a division of Collaborative Investment Ltd, we leverage years of experience in property management, agribusiness, and industrial services to deliver end-to-end solar solutions. We don't just sell solar products — we start, manage, and scale solar energy across all our sectors.

Today, CIL Solar powers farms, hotels, IT companies, betting shops, homes, and diaspora-managed properties. We partner with leading manufacturers to bring the best solar technology to Nigeria and help our partners scale their businesses.

Powering Nigeria's future with solar energy
